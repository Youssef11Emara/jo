Features

  • This phone is the global version of the Redmi Note 12 Turbo that was announced in China.
  • The phone supports NFC and IR Blaster.
  • The phone supports two Nano SIM cards.
  • Supports 2G, 3G, 4G and 5G networks.
  • The phone has dimensions of 161.1 x 75 x 7.9 mm and weighs 181 grams.
  • Back material is plastic.
  • The phone comes with IP53 water resistance.
  • The screen comes in the form of a hole in the middle of the screen and comes in the AMOLED type and comes with an area of ​​​​6.67 inches with a resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels at a pixel density rate of 395 pixels per inch with the phone supporting a refresh rate of 120Hz in addition to the presence of a protective layer on the phone screen of the type Corning Gorilla Glass 5 with support for the Dolby Vision feature and HDR10 + with a brightness of up to 1000 candles supporting 68 billion colors.
  • The phone is available in more than one version of solid memory and random memory as follows: - The first version comes with a solid memory of 256 GB of type UFS 3.1 with a random memory of 8 GB.
  • The second version comes with a solid memory of 256 GB of UFS 3.1 type with a random memory of 12 GB.
  • The phone does not support the ability to increase storage space by installing a memory card.
  • The phone comes with a Qualcomm Snapdragon 7+ Gen 2 processor with 4nm technology and an Adreno 725 graphics processor.
  • The front camera comes with a resolution of 16 megapixels and an F/2.5 lens aperture.
  • The rear camera comes with a triple camera, where the first camera comes with a resolution of 64 megapixels with an F/1.8 lens aperture that supports the OIS optical stabilizer, and it is the main camera. As for the second camera, it comes with a resolution of 8 megapixels with an F/2.2 lens aperture, and it is the camera for wide photography. As for the third camera, it comes with a resolution of 2 megapixels with an F/2.4 lens aperture, and it is the camera for macro photography, in addition to a single LED flash.
  • The phone supports shooting videos in 4K quality at a resolution of 2160 pixels at a capture rate of 30 frames per second, and it also supports shooting in FHD quality at a resolution of 1080 pixels at a capture rate of 30, 60 and 120 frames per second.
  • Supports an additional microphone for noise isolation.
  • Wi-Fi comes in a/b/g/n/ac/6 frequencies and supports Dual-band.
  • The phone supports Bluetooth version 5.3 with A2DP, LE features.
  • The phone supports GPS geolocation with other navigation systems such as GLONASS, BDS, GALILEO, QZSS.
  • Security and protection methods supported by the phone: The phone supports a fingerprint sensor and comes integrated with the power button. It also supports the Face Unlock feature to unlock the phone using the face.
  • The phone supports most of the sensors of acceleration, proximity, gyroscope and compass. The external speakers come with stereo sound.
  • The phone supports a 3.5 mm headphone jack.
  • The USB port is Type C and supports OTG.
  • The battery has a capacity of 5000 mAh and supports 67W fast charging.
  • The phone comes with Android 13 operating system with the latest Xiaomi interface MIUI 14 dedicated to Poco.
  • The phone is available in black, blue and white.

Flaws

  1. It does not support the ability to install a memory card, and you will not need it as the phone's storage space is large.
  2. The back of the phone is made of plastic, not glass like the previous Poco F4.
  3. Actual screen brightness under the sun is up to 1000 nits but decreases quickly.
  4. With pressure on the processor, it reduces its frequencies and reduces the brightness of the screen, but maintains strong performance.
  5. Modest performance from the selfie camera as usual.
  6. In general, the development in the Poco F5 over the previous generation is in the use of a newer processor and a larger battery.
